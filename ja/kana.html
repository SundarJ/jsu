    • +dakuten marker: k→g, ts/s→z, t→d, h→b, ch/sh→j

    +handakuten marker: h→p

    small ゃゅょ: added to kana ending in -i, truncates sound (きゃ→kya, not kiya)

    small ぃ: used to form sounds foreign to Japanese, such as ふぃ, fi.

    small っ: indicates that the following consonant is doubled—さか:saka, さっか:sakka

    ゝ/ヽ/々→iteration mark (佐々木→sasaki)

    一: used to stretch out the preceding kana (メエル (email)→メール)
